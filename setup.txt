## вҡ пёҸ TROUBLESHOOTING

### Camera not opening
- Check `/dev/video*` permissions (Linux)
- Try different index inside `open_camera()`

### Camera not closing
- Press `q` in OpenCV window
- OR ensure `cap.release()` is called

### No detection happening
- Ensure proper lighting
- Keep hand inside frame
- Check if model.pkl is trained correctly

### Streamlit camera not working
- Use `streamlit-webrtc` for browser camera
- OpenCV camera works only locally
